Our Products

We offer a wide range welded wire mesh fences which can suit multiple applications demanding excellent perimeter protection & longevity. Our products boast many features like aesthetics, anti-climb, anti-cut and anti-break, anti-corrode, which makes them suitable for protecting high value assets. Weldmesh fences consist of high quality steel wires welded together forming a mesh structure which creates a strong steel barrier. Weldmesh fences are more rigid as compared to woven fences which adds to high security. Rigid panels help in easy installation as there are hardly any horizontal loads on the poles as compared to woven fences & also require very few accessories for installation. All our weldmesh products are coated in state of the art coating facilities making sure that all the international standards are adhered to so that the finish looks beautiful for a very long time. Consistent high quality welding helps in maintaining the weld strength & mesh size throughout the fence.

GI (Galvanized Iron) Welded Wiremesh

This weld mesh is a mesh made by welding wires usually placed perpendicular to each other forming rectangular or square shapes. It finds applications in many areas - cable trays, fence, machine guards, grills, etc. It is a rigid fabric, giving it a neat appearance. In the fence application, by adjusting the mesh sizes it can become a potent security barrier.

MS (Mild Steel) Welded Wiremesh

Mild Steel Weld mesh is made of wire hard drawn from controlled quality mild steel wire rods. This weld mesh is a mesh made by welding wires usually placed perpendicular to each other forming rectangular or square shapes. Wires generally used are of wire diameter 2.00mm to 6.00mm. It finds applications in many areas - cable trays, fence, machine guards, grills, etc. It is also extensively used in Structural Slabs, Pre-Cast Members & RCC Pipes, Floor & Pavement Slabs & Grating. It is a rigid fabric, giving it a neat appearance. In the fence application, by adjusting the mesh sizes it can become a potent security barrier.
